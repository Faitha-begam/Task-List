Iron Man

Iron Man is a genius billionaire who fights using advanced armored suits. Tony Stark is known for his intelligence, technology, and leadership.

Thor

Thor is the God of Thunder from Asgard who uses the magical hammer Mjolnir. He is famous for his strength and lightning powers.

Captain America

Captain America is a super soldier known for his bravery and powerful shield. Steve Rogers represents leadership, courage, and justice.

Hulk

Hulk transforms from Bruce Banner into a giant green superhero with immense strength. His power increases whenever he becomes angry.

Spider-Man

Spider-Man uses spider-like abilities to protect people and fight villains. Peter Parker is loved for his humor, agility, and web-slinging skills.
