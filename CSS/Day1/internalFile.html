Tirunelveli Halwa is a soft and glossy sweet made using wheat milk and ghee. It is one of the most iconic traditional sweets in Tamil Nadu.

Ambur Biryani is one of Tamil Nadu’s most famous spicy rice dishes. It is popular for its unique aroma and soft seeraga samba rice.

Chettinad Chicken is famous for its rich spices and flavorful masala. It is one of the spiciest and most popular dishes in South India.

Tamil Nadu filter coffee is loved for its strong aroma and authentic taste. It is traditionally served in steel tumblers.

Jigarthanda is a famous Madurai drink made using milk, ice cream, and badam pisin. It is a refreshing summer beverage loved across Tamil Nadu.
